Garden Clicker

Description

"Garden Clicker" is an immersive 2D game that requires players to accumulate Garden Coins (GC) while protecting them from malicious bugs that frequently appear on the screen. To safeguard your precious coins and ten hearts, you must swiftly click on the bugs and vanquish them before they can cause any harm. The game also allows for an exciting challenge of speedrunning, where players aim to max out all of the available upgrades before completing the game. With vibrant graphics, immersive sound effects, and engaging gameplay mechanics, "Garden Clicker" provides an enjoyable and addictive gaming experience for players of all ages.
